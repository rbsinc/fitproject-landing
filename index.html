Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who actually train. Fast logging, smart generation, real progress tracking.

Workout Logger

Log sets, reps, weight, and rest periods. Track warm-up sets separately. See your personal records and history for every exercise right where you need them.

Smart Workout Generation

AI-powered Smart Rotation creates routines from your profile — experience level, goals, equipment, and schedule. Push, pull, legs — it handles the programming.

Progress Tracking

Strength curves, personal records, weekly volume, active time, and streaks. See how you're actually progressing with data from your real sessions.

Equipment Config

Tell the app exactly what you have — dumbbells with weight ranges, barbells with plates, cables, bench, pull-up bar. Workouts only use what's available to you.

Reps in Reserve

Track effort with RIR after each exercise — from max effort to very easy. The app uses this to understand your intensity and adjust future suggestions.

How it works

Set up once. Train forever.

One minute of setup gives you personalized workouts for as long as you train.

01

Configure your profile

Set your experience level, primary goal (strength, hypertrophy, or endurance), weekly frequency, session duration, and available equipment. Takes about a minute.

02

Generate or build

Use Smart Rotation to auto-generate a weekly routine, or create your own templates. Either way, just hit Start Workout when you're ready.

03

Log and progress

Track every set during your session. Log your reps in reserve. Watch your strength curves climb. The more data you give it, the smarter your workouts become.

FAQ

Common questions

Straight answers.

Is Fit Project really free?+
Yes. Fit Project is free to use — no subscription, no paywall on core features. It was built as a passion project by a developer who was tired of paying monthly fees just to log workouts. The workout logging and smart generation features you see today are free, and the goal is to keep it that way.
How does Smart Rotation work?+
Smart Rotation generates your weekly workout plan based on your profile settings — experience level, primary goal, available equipment, weekly frequency (2-7 days), and session duration (15-120 minutes). It creates a balanced split (push, pull, lower body, etc.) and rotates through it, factoring in what muscle groups you've recently trained and your progression over time.
What can I track during a workout?+
For each exercise you can track sets (including warm-up sets), reps, weight, and rest periods. After completing an exercise, you rate your effort using Reps in Reserve (RIR). The app also tracks your total session time, total volume, personal records, and shows your strength curve progression over time.
What data do you collect?+
Fit Project is privacy-first. Your workout data is never sold to advertisers or shared with third parties. Your fitness data is yours alone.
Can I use my own workouts instead of generated ones?+
Absolutely. You can create custom workout templates, save completed workouts as templates, and build your own weekly routine from scratch. The AI generation is there when you want it, but you're never locked into it.
What equipment is supported?+
Fit Project supports bench, barbell (with plate configuration), dumbbells (with custom weight ranges), kettlebells, cables, pull-up bar, and more. You toggle what you have, specify details like your dumbbell range, and the app only generates workouts using equipment available to you.
What platforms is Fit Project available on?+
Fit Project is launching on both iOS (Apple App Store) and Android (Google Play Store). Both versions are completely free.
