## What is Fit Project?

Fit Project is a mobile workout app built for people who want to train consistently without paying $15–30/month for basic features. It started as a personal tool — built by a developer who wanted a simple, private way to log workouts and get smart routines — and is now available for everyone.

## Features

**Workout Logger** — Log sets, reps, weight, and rest periods with a fast interface designed for use at the gym. Track warm-up sets separately and see your personal records for every exercise.

**Smart Workout Generation** — AI-powered Smart Rotation creates personalized routines based on your experience level, goals (strength, hypertrophy, endurance), weekly frequency (2–7 days), session duration (15–120 min), and available equipment. It generates balanced push/pull/legs splits and adapts to your training history.

**Progress Tracking** — Strength curves over time, personal records, weekly volume, active training time, and weekly streaks. Real data from real sessions.

**Equipment Configuration** — Tell the app exactly what you have: dumbbells with weight ranges, barbells with plates, cables, bench, pull-up bar, and more. Workouts only use equipment available to you.

**Reps in Reserve (RIR)** — Rate your effort after each exercise from max effort to very easy. The app uses this to understand your intensity and adjust future suggestions.

**Custom Templates** — Create your own workout templates, save completed workouts as templates, or build a weekly routine from scratch. Use AI generation when you want it, skip it when you don't.

## Privacy

Fit Project is privacy-first. Your workout data is not sold to advertisers, shared with third parties, or used for tracking. There are no analytics trackers, no ads, no data brokers. Your fitness data is yours alone.

## Availability

Fit Project is coming soon on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). Both versions are completely free.

## Why Free?

This isn't a startup. There's no venture capital, no monetization plan, no premium tier waiting behind a paywall. Fit Project is a passion project built by someone who uses it every day and wants to share it with others who feel the same frustration with overpriced fitness apps.
